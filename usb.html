Alaska Lite 11

Alaska Lite 11 is a passion project created by Dario Simpson to bring a better Windows 11 experience. it started just by removing bloat from a default installation and then try to automate the process for multiple machines, one thing lead to another, and then I decided to create Alaska Lite 11 version of Windows and distributed as freeware over the internet.
